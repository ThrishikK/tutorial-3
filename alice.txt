The Vindhya Range is a complex, discontinuous chain of mountain ridges
The Vindhyas have a great significance in Indian mythology and history.
Several ancient texts mention the Vindhyas as the southern boundary of the Āryāvarta, the territory of the ancient Indo-Aryan peoples.
